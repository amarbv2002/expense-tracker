EXPENSE TRACKER

How this app works?
Ever calculated your expenses? Always thinking of tracking your expenses? If you have answered No and Yes, respectively, then you have come to the right place.

Now let's understand how this application works.
  • Add a new transaction by entering the name and amount of the transaction.
  • Click on the Add Income button if it's an income for you. Click on the Add Expense button if it's an expense for you.
  • Your balance, income, expenses and transaction history will update according to your entered data.
  • If you want to delete an entry, then simply click the red X button and the entry will be deleted and the new balance sheet will be updated.
  • The data you enter will be saved in the local storage of your browser. So don't worry if you have closed the window or the tab. The data will be saved until you clear all the data in your browser.
This app is built using Vanilla JavaScript and the Bootstrap Framework. If you find any bugs, please let me know.
